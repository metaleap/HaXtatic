Some contents require repetetive use of quickly-becoming-too-verbose "mostly just inner text" tags such as {X|c: <blockquote> |}, {X|c: <code> |}, or {X|c: <p class="my-para-highlighted my-smallerfont"> |} etc.

Setting up hax.{P|fileBaseName|} X-tags for any such instances alleviates this quickly, and lets the author turn repetetive-hence-too-verbose occurrences of:

  • {X|c: <blockquote>content</blockquote> |} into {X|c: {X|bq:content|} |}
  • {X|c: <code>content</code> |} into {X|c: {X|c:content|} |}
  • {X|c: <p class="my-para-highlighted my-smallerfont">content</p> |} into {X|c: {X|phs:content|} |}
  • Et cetera!
