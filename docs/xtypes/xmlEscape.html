hax.xmlEscape

XML/HTML-escapes all occurrences of & < > " ' in the given content.

In a nutshell

  1. Declaration of an |X|hax.xmlEscape:myXtag directive in a *.haxproj file,
    • further configuring: (no settings)
  2. Usage anywhere: {X|myXtag: any "content' to <> XML&HTML-escape |}
