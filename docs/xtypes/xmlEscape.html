Stage:

Early — this X-renderer does not require a page context for rendering: therefore any hax.xmlEscape-invoking X-tags encountered during pre-templating (at start-up time) will be processed eagerly (for reduced per-page processing load).

Purpose

As a general principle, none of the built-in X-renderers ever HTML/XML-escape, of their own accord, their generated output derived from custom inputs: users are expected to specify such inputs already-escaped as the project's / file's output format demands.

As such, some projects might for example require XML/HTML-escaping other haXtag outputs dynamically/selectively after the fact: and this is where xmlEscape offers a handy way out.
