hax.htmlLinks

Outputs a sequence of <a ..> .. </a> or  <outerhtmltag ..><a ..> .. </a></outerhtmltag> in a uniform manner.

In a nutshell

  1. Declaration of an |X|hax.htmlLinks:myTag:outerhtmltag: .. directive in a *.haxproj file,
    • further configuring: attr, itemsFirst, itemsLast, wrapHref
  2. Usage anywhere: {X|myTag: items = [..] , attr = [..] |}

Stage:

Early or Page — this X-renderer determines dynamically whether or not it requires a page context for rendering: so some hax.htmlLinks-invoking X-tags encountered during pre-templating (at start-up time) may be processed eagerly (for overall-reduced per-page processing load), and others may be skipped over and thus delayed until page time — depending on the X-tag's configuration and/or parameters.
