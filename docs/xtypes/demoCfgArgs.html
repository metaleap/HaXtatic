hax.demoCfgArgs

Outputs <h1>Hello, greetname!</h1>.

In a nutshell

  1. Declaration of an |X|hax.demoCfgArgs:myXtag or |X|hax.demoCfgArgs:myXtag:greetname directive in a *.haxproj file,
    • further configuring: (no other settings)
  2. Usage anywhere: {X|myXtag|} or {X|myXtag:greetname |}

This "slightly pointless" X-renderer exists solely to serve as a code sample to start from for Haskell programmers wanting to develop custom X-renderers within the HaXtatic code-base.
