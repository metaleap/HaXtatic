Stage:

Early or Page — this X-renderer determines dynamically whether or not it requires a page context for rendering: so some hax.repeat-invoking X-tags encountered during pre-templating (at start-up time) may be processed eagerly (for reduced per-page processing load), and others may be skipped over and thus delayed until page time — depending on the X-tag's configuration and/or parameters.
