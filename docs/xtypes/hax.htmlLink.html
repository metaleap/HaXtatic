hax.htmlLink

Outputs <a ..>..</a>.

In a nutshell

  1. Declaration of an |X|hax.htmlLink:myTag:some/rel/base/path: .. directive in a *.haxproj file,
    • further configuring: attr , xmlEscape , uriAutoExt
  2. Usage anywhere: {X|myTag:rel-to-base/path/no/whitespace and then the link text text |}

Stage:

Early — this X-renderer does not require a page context for rendering: therefore any hax.htmlLink-invoking X-tags encountered during pre-templating (at start-up time) will be processed eagerly (for overall-reduced per-page processing load).
